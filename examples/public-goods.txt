Public Goods Game

5 agents each start with 100 tokens. Each round, every agent simultaneously decides how many tokens to contribute to a public fund (between 0 and their current balance). The total contributions are multiplied by 1.5 and then divided equally among all 5 agents, regardless of how much each contributed.

Agents keep whatever they did not contribute plus their share of the multiplied public fund.

If the total contribution across all agents drops below 5 tokens for 3 consecutive rounds, the cooperation has collapsed and the simulation ends early.

Run for 30 rounds. Each agent can observe their own balance and the total contributions from the previous round. They cannot see individual contributions from other agents.
